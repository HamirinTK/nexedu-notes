# Welcome to Mathematics

Mathematics is the foundation of many disciplines. Whether you're diving into **calculus**, **linear algebra**, **statistics**, or **number theory**, this section gathers all the resources you need to learn and practice.

## 📚 What you'll find here

- **Calculus** – limits, derivatives, integrals, and beyond.
- **Algebra** – from basic equations to abstract structures.
- **Statistics & Probability** – data analysis and inference.
- **Geometry & Topology** – shapes, spaces, and transformations.

## 📖 How to use this space

Each topic (e.g., Calculus) is further divided into **sub‑topics** and **lessons**.  
Use the sidebar navigation or the homepage topic selector to jump straight into any lesson.
