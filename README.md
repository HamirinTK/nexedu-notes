## What is this?
NEXEDU is a global, open-access educational ecosystem. By providing freely accessible academic resources across a diverse range of acedemic fields, we empower students, educators, and lifelong learners to delve into their curioisty without boundaries.

## Purpose
We believe that financial barriers should never limit intellectual growth or human potential. Access to "quality" learning materials is a fundamental right, not a privilege. NEXEDU was built to foster a global community driven by curiosity, collaboration, and shared knowledge.
